## Section 1: Major Recent Pump-and-Dump Incidents

### The July 2025 Chinese Micro-Cap Collapse

**Seven US-listed Chinese stocks crashed 80-93%** in July 2025 after being aggressively promoted on WhatsApp and social media:

| Stock | Peak → Crash | Loss |
|-------|-------------|------|
| **Ostin Technology (OST)** | ~$1B market cap → -93% | June 26, 2025 |
| **Top KingWin (WAI)** | Pumped → -90% | July 7, 2025 |
| **Park Ha Biological (PHH)** | Pumped → -93% | July 8, 2025 |
| **Concorde International (CIGL)** | Pumped → -80% | July 10, 2025 |
| **Everbright Digital (EDHL)** | Pumped → -80% | July 14, 2025 |
| **Skyline Builders** | Pumped → -80%+ | July 2025 |
| **Pheton Holdings** | Pumped → -80%+ | July 2025 |

**Total estimated losses:** $3.7 billion

**Promotion methods documented:**
- WhatsApp groups posing as US investment firms
- Facebook ads driving victims to private groups
- Reddit accounts promoting in coordinated bursts (12 accounts promoting OST in same 2-hour window)
- Accounts geolocated to Russia and Iran involved in promotion

**Victim testimony:** "I lost all my savings after joining a WhatsApp group from a Facebook ad. The group admins claimed to be a US investment firm." — Tia Castagno (UK)

**Legal response:** UK law firm representing 100+ victims of Chinese penny stock schemes.

### How Modern Pump Schemes Operate

Based on our research, the typical flow is:

```
1. Facebook Ad → "Free investment tips from expert"
           ↓
2. WhatsApp/Telegram Group → Victims added to "exclusive" chat
           ↓
3. Build Trust → Fake profits shown, testimonials
           ↓
4. The Pump → Group told to buy specific stock NOW
           ↓
5. Reddit/Stocktwits → Coordinated posts create buzz
           ↓
6. Price Spikes → FOMO drives more retail buying
           ↓
7. The Dump → Promoters sell at peak
           ↓
8. Collapse → 80-95% crash in hours/days
```

## Section 4: SEC/FINRA Enforcement Activity

### SEC Cross-Border Task Force (September 2025)

The SEC announced a dedicated task force specifically targeting:
- Foreign-based companies' market manipulation
- Pump-and-dump schemes involving Chinese issuers
- "Bilge bracket" gatekeepers (small underwriters, auditors, law firms)

**Quote from SEC:** "Particular attention will be paid to China and other foreign jurisdictions where governmental control and other factors pose unique investor risks."

### Recent Enforcement Actions

| Case | Date | Amount | Description |
|------|------|--------|-------------|
| **$214M forfeiture** | 2024-2025 | $214M | Chinese individuals posing as US advisors on social media |
| **$110M OST fraud** | 2025 | $110M | Chinese executives indicted for coordinated social media pump |
| **@BigMoneyMike6** | 2024 | $3.6M | Twitter promoter with 3M followers charged with scalping |
| **AI Wealth/Lane Wealth** | Dec 2025 | $14M | Crypto investment clubs using WhatsApp |
| **9 Investment Advisers** | Sept 2024 | $1.24M | Marketing rule violations on social media |

### FINRA Investigations

FINRA is actively investigating broker-dealers for:
- IPOs raising ≤$25M with share prices $4-$8
- Offerings from January 2023 - September 2025
- Companies from high-risk overseas jurisdictions

### Nasdaq New Requirements (September 2025)

- Companies from high-risk jurisdictions must maintain **$25M minimum public float**
- Net income companies need **$15M minimum public float value**
- Expedited delisting for companies with market value <$5M

## Section 6: Regulatory Warnings

### SEC Investor Alerts (2025)

The SEC has issued specific warnings about:

1. **Group Chat Scams** (December 2025)
   - Fraudsters use WhatsApp, Telegram for "exclusive" investment tips
   - Fake financial gurus, professors, CEOs lead groups
   - AI-powered trading bots promoted

2. **SEC Impersonation Scams** (September 2025)
   - Fraudsters impersonate SEC officials on social media
   - Stock tips, advance fee fraud, "recovery" scams

3. **Fraudulent Stock Promotions** (Updated 2025)
   - Social media promotion without disclosure is illegal
   - Microcap stocks especially vulnerable
   - Section 17(b) requires compensation disclosure

### FINRA Warnings

- Small companies with overseas operations are "especially vulnerable" to pump-and-dumps
- Warning issued since 2022, intensified in 2025
- Focus on companies with limited US presence

## Section 7: Conclusions and Recommendations

### Key Conclusions

1. **Our flagged stocks match the manipulation profile** - The 110 micro-cap stocks identified by ScamDunk share identical characteristics with stocks involved in the $3.7B July 2025 collapse.

2. **Active promotion infrastructure exists** - Large Discord/Telegram communities, coordinated Reddit posting, and WhatsApp schemes are actively targeting retail investors.

3. **Regulatory response is ramping up** - SEC Cross-Border Task Force, Nasdaq listing requirements, and FINRA investigations all launched in 2025.

4. **Several flagged stocks show active promotion** - SIDU, SOBR, OPTX, and others appear on social media watchlists with unusual activity.

5. **Chinese holding companies are primary targets** - Both our analysis and SEC enforcement focus on US-listed Chinese micro-caps.

### Investor Protection Recommendations

1. **Avoid stocks promoted in group chats** - WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord "tips" are high-risk
2. **Verify information independently** - Don't rely on social media for investment decisions
3. **Check SEC EDGAR filings** - Limited disclosure is a red flag
4. **Be wary of Chinese micro-caps** - Regulatory scrutiny is intensifying
5. **Report suspected fraud** - SEC tips: sec.gov/tcr, FBI: ic3.gov

### For Regulators/Journalists

The stocks flagged by ScamDunk represent a current, active threat to retail investors. Our AI detected the same patterns that resulted in $3.7 billion in losses in July 2025. The 24 stocks showing BOTH pump-and-dump patterns AND volume explosions warrant immediate investigation.
