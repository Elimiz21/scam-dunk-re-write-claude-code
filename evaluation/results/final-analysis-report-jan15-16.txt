Risk scoring uses 8 quantitative signals:
  1. HIGH_VOLATILITY: Daily price volatility > 50%
  2. MICROCAP_PRICE: Stock price below $5 (penny stock territory)
  3. MICRO_LIQUIDITY: Daily dollar volume below $500K
  4. SPIKE_7D: >100% price movement in 7 days
  5. SPIKE_THEN_DROP: Pump-and-dump pattern (rapid rise + fall)

Persistent HIGH risk across multiple dates indicates sustained suspicious activity.
