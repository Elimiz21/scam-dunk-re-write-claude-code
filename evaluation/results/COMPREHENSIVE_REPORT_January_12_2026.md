## Part 5: Warning Signs for Investors

### Red Flags Identified Across Promoted Stocks

1. **Market Cap Under $50M** - Easy to manipulate
2. **Stock Price Under $2** - Penny stock volatility
3. **Limited Float** - Enables rapid price spikes
4. **Chinese/International Holding Companies** - Less regulatory oversight
5. **Minimal Analyst Coverage** - No independent verification
6. **Recent Reverse Splits** - Often indicates distress
7. **Coordinated Social Media Alerts** - Artificial demand creation

### How to Identify Pump-and-Dump Schemes

**Per SEC/Investor.gov:**
1. Unsolicited stock tips via SMS, email, social media
2. Claims of guaranteed returns or "insider information"
3. Pressure to act quickly before "missing out"
4. Obscure companies with limited public information
5. Promoters not disclosing they were paid to promote
6. Aggressive online promotion on forums, social media, chatrooms
